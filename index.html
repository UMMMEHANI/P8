HORSE RIDING
Horse riding is a sport and hobby that involves controlling a horse's movement and speed. It can be practiced in a riding school or in the open air, and can be done alone or with others.
Horse riding
1.What it is
The art of riding a horse and controlling its movement and speed
2.Benefits
Improves muscle tone and balance, builds confidence, and can boost mood
3.History
Started over 5,000 years ago, and the horse has played a crucial role in many human societies
4.Other names
Equestrianism, horseback riding, or riding
5.Related activities
Driving and vaulting
Horse riding can be a great way to get out in nature and spend time with animals. It can also be a meditative and joy-inducing experience.
To become a good rider, you can learn to: Recognize horse behavior, Communicate with horses using body language and voice, and Groom horses and clean their environment.
While horse riding can contribute to glute development, genetics, diet, and overall physical activity also play a role in determining muscle size and appearance.
